The Rules

1. Membership is open to all players, family, partners, local community and businesses. Basically anybody!

2. All payments must be by direct debit to the Scissett FC account IN ADVANCE.

3. Prizes are drawn each month and paid out in cash.

4. The scheme runs for the full year with no break.

5. No one member can win the top prize in two consecutive months, but can win other prize.

6. Scissett FC reserve the right at its sole disretion to amend the rules of the scheme at any time.

7. By entering the scheme, you agree to abide by all the rules stated herein.

8. The 1902 club prize draw is classed as gambling. You must be over 18 to play. For more information on gambling, or to be more gamble aware, visit the Be Gamble Aware® website.
