Scissett FC Annual (Very) Amateur Golf Day 2019

Sunday 29th September, from 9am at Midgley Golf Course

Calling all amateur golfers, fundraisers and general friends of Scissett FC – back by popular demand, our annual golf day invites you to spend the morning tearing up the golf course with friends and family of Scissett FC.

This annual event, which is again being hosted by our friends at Midgley Golf Course, will see you enjoy some fresh air, interspersed with hitting (or attempting to hit) a small round ball with a stick, surrounded by beautiful scenery for 9 magical holes of golfing greatness.

Whether a serious golfer, or an avid fan of a walking, this event is aimed to provide fun for everyone.

The golf is preceded by bacon butties on arrival and a complimentary drink after the round, with arrivals from 8am and first tee offs at 9am (tee times will be published before the round).

There will be an array of prizes on offer, including the prestigious wooden spoon which our very own Tom Jones has firmly placed on his mantle piece have secured this coveted title 2 years on the bounce.

Costs for the day are £25 per person, to include bacon butties, golf and a complimentary drink.

To book your place please contact Richard Sterling (07851279616) or Tom Jones (07523499476).
