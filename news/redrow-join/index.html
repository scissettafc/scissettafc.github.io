Welcome to our New Partner Redrow Homes!

We are very excited to welcome to our commercial partner network, Redrow Homes. Redrow are working hard in the local Scissett community with their brand new Langley Grange development and are keen to further invest in the local community as part of this project.

As a sponsor, Redrow will be the main clubhouse and pitch partner, with lots of coverage in the clubhouse and behind the bottom goal at the Nortonthorpe pitch. There will also be lots of literature and information available on the bar after every match, for our fans and players to find out more about the new homes being built.

"Scissett are happy to bring Redrow on board, and we are delighted that such a large development company have chosen to support us in our aims. When I sat down with the guys at Redrow's head office, we immediately understood what Scissett can aim for and achieve in the next few years. I believe this is the start of a fantastic working relationship, and I look forward to seeing how we can help Redrow grow in the Scissett community, as well as how they can support us." - Nick Sterling, Chairman
